Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

ASKdb ("the App") is developed and operated by an independent developer. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data Collection

ASKdb does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The App runs entirely on your device. We do not operate any backend service that receives your data.

2. Database Credentials

Connection details you enter (host, port, username, password, SSH keys) are stored locally on your device using macOS/iOS secure storage mechanisms. They are never sent to us or any third party.

3. AI / Natural Language Processing

When you use the natural language query feature, your prompt and database schema metadata are sent to your configured AI service provider (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic) to generate SQL. The App sends only the minimum context required:

  • Your natural language question
  • Table and column names (schema structure)

Your actual database content (row data) is never sent to any AI provider. Please review your chosen AI provider's privacy policy for how they handle API requests.

4. Network Access

The App requires network access for:

  • Database connections — connecting to remote MySQL, PostgreSQL, or SQLite databases over TCP
  • SSH tunneling — establishing encrypted SSH tunnels for secure access to databases on private networks (local port forwarding on 127.0.0.1 only)
  • AI service API calls — sending natural language queries to your configured AI provider

5. Microphone Access

The App may request microphone access to support voice input for natural language queries. Audio is processed on-device for speech recognition and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted.

6. File Access

The App accesses files you explicitly select (SQLite databases, SSH private keys) using macOS security-scoped bookmarks. It does not scan or access files beyond what you grant.

7. Analytics and Tracking

ASKdb does not include any analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or tracking tools. No usage data is collected.

8. Third-Party Services

The only external communication is with the AI service provider you configure. No data is shared with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third party.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
